Day 1: Shipping the Signals Engine

Apr 12, 2026

Today the pipeline crossed the threshold: every new crazy idea can be routed, built, seeded, and validated with zero babysitting. The result is a nightly signals engine that precomputes a sector‑level verdict for every S&P 500 ticker.

What ships tonight: new algos generated daily, auto‑seeded, and a static JSON cache under docs/signals/ that powers the stock lookup UI.

The product angle is simple: enter a ticker, see if the weird signals agree with the sector. It’s not advice; it’s a transparent experiment with receipts.

Next steps: publish the lookup UI, keep the data flowing, and let the leaderboard evolve without intervention. The consistency is the moat, not the code.

This is an experimental signal lab. Not financial advice. Signals can and will fail. Do your own research. Full disclaimer.
